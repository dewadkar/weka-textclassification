            The 2000 Crown Victoria is a 4-door, 6-passenger full-size sedan available in two trims, the Sedan and LX. The Crown Victoria's closest competitors include the Buick LeSabre, Toyota Avalon and Chrysler Concorde. Upon introduction, both trims are equipped with a standard 4.6-liter, V8, 200-horsepower engine that consumes 18-mpg in the city and 25-mpg on the highway. An electronically controlled 4-speed automatic transmission with overdrive is standard on both trims. The 2000 Crown Victoria is a carryover from 1999 with added interior and exterior colors.Major Standard Features: 
Air conditioning, Driver side airbag, Passenger side airbag, Anti-lock brakes, Anti-theft vehicle, Cruise control, Rear defogger, Keyless entry unlock, Power locks, Power mirrors, Power steering, Power windows, AM/FM radio, Cassette, Driver power seats, Tilt steering, Automatic transmission 

Major Optional Features: 
Alloy wheels, CD player, Passenger power seats, Leather seats, Traction control
