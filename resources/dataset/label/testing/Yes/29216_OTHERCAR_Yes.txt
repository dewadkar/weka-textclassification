            2000 Lincoln Town Car Signature 4-Door Sedan 4.6.  The ease of driving this car in the best I have ever driven.  With a comprehensive list of standard features and is available in three models, the executive, signature and cartier. Standard features for all models include traction control, a remote keyless entry system, cruise control and a tilt steering wheel. Rear air conditioning, an anti-lock brake system, power windows, power door locks and an eight-way power driver seat are also standard. Optional features available are a power moonroof, a portable/cellular telephone and a CD changer.  Key enhancements for this year include the reinstatement of a storage armrest in the front passenger side door and upgraded tires. Also new is a Signature Touring Package that improves suspension and a restyled exterior and interior. <br><br>
