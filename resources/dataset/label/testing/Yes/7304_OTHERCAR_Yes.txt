            The user-friendly D-460 digital zoom camera offers 1.31 million pixel resolution CCD with a 3x optical/2x digital telephoto zoom lens and even more convenient features than its popular predecessors. Housed in a compact and stylish metallic silver body, it features a 6-mode pop-up intelligent flash, customizable advanced-level settings, easy viewing with its 1.8-inch color LCD monitor, burst mode shooting and autofocus. Improvements over previous models include easier-to-use controls and function buttons, new Olympus lithium batteries for longer life and a wider range of image compression modes.
