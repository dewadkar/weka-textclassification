            This could very well be Chevy's best truck.  General Motors has put a lot into this truck to improve the comfort, ride and power.  The result are amazing.  With the standard features included  in the package, this truck can sell itself.

INTERIOR
Very spacious and comfortable form front to rear.  The front seats are power adjusting which feature  a lumbar support for the lower back. the rear seat fold up for more cargo room. In addition, it pulls out at an angle to allow better comfort for seating.  The dash board controls are within easy reach of the driver.  A computer monitor all the systems (such as oil life, fuel, ABS, et) and display  information on the message board when needed. Tilt wheel tinted glass, dual airbags, Intermittent wipers, CD-AM/FM radio, AC, a security system are all standard features.

EXTERIOR
The step side body is very flashes and sporty.  This style can easily be customized to anybody's taste. There a vary of colors to choose from. The truck features front and rear chrome bumpers, large tail lights, daytime running lights, Extended cab with four door entry, protective cover on the truck's bed, Trailer hitch package,  remote keyless entry.

UNDER THE HOOD
This model has the Vortec 5300 V8 SFI gas engine.  It gets 433 miles to a 25 gallon tank full.  This is the bigger engine of the two V8 engines.  Enought power for just about any job.  All the fluids, such as oil, brake, washer, coolant, etc. are color coded.  One belt controls everything, That include alternator, air conditioner, power steering, the fly wheel.

THE RIDE
Because of the improve suspension system, The big V8 engine, and the seat package this truck perform much better than many of its competitors - REAL COMFORT.  No air noises and very hard to hear sounds from the outside. Steering and control are effortless.  This truck really perform well on the road.
