            The BMW 318 series is truely a work of art. It is the ultimate driving machine and gives optimal performance. It is what everyone would dream of having in a car at an affordable price. Available in a variety of colors ( silver, black, red, blue, white, gun metallic, etc) this car is truely a novelty to drive. Its powerful 1.9 liter turbo charged engine makes it seem that one is driving a sports car. The car comes with a variety of extra options and gizmos such as power windows, anti-theft systems, powered moon roof, bose music speaker system etc. the interior of the car is also very room, spacious and comfortable and even though it is a 5 seater I have managed to fit 6 people in it quite comfortabley. The car also has a very decent fuel efficieny and that combined with high quality comfort, safety and performace makes this car ideal for all kids of people.<br><br>
