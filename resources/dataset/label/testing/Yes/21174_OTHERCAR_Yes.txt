             If you are looking for an incredible luxurious car, the Lexus Gs-400 is the one. This car is excellent. It is the fastest automatic in the world, and has had several votes for the car of the year. No wonder, the luxury is incredible, and the driving is smooth. When ever you drive on the road, you feel like your flying because of the smooth engine. My family chose this car because we were looking for an incredibly luxurious car that we can depend on for a low price, and yes indeed it is. The car starts off at $35,000. And for such a very nice car, thats an excellent price.For Package Options you can get on this car are The Navigational System Package, or the Nakamishi Stereo System Package. You can also get heated seats, Zeenon Lights, and Rear Windshield Wiper, and many more. The car consists of 10 Boze Speakers, each with 80 Wattz. Over all I highly reccomend this car to people around 22-45 years of age.<br><br>
