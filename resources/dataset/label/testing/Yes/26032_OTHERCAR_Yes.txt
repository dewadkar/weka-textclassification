              This was my first real auto purchase, since I had leased previously. As bad as it might sound, I didn't research this car at all before purchasing it. I actually went to look at the Intrepid, found that it was a little pricier than I could afford, and then settled on the Stratus, which I had never even heard of before. 
  I bought this car about a month ago, and so far I must say that I am very pleased with the Stratus. It is quite simply an attractive, reliable car at a great price. I would have liked a sportier vehicle, yet after choosing the Stratus based on more economical concerns, I have found that it is much more than I expected stylewise. I have gotten numerous compliments on it, and compared to other similarly priced cars, I would say that it is a very aesthetically pleasing vehicle.
