            I bought my  1991 Chrysler Town &amp; Country in Athens Georgia and I love it it has comfortable Lumbar seats for travel, It has plenty of room for families, and it is a nice driving van.I like the way it travels and it is terrific on gas when taking trips. The mini van also has child safety locks on the sliding door and it is safe for kids and women driving alone at night. All the doors automatically lock when you get up to a certain speed. This is one feature that really is great if you are single and travel alone at night. My van sits seven and still has plenty of room for luggage or groceries. I haven't had too much wrong with my van since I got it. The air bag is another good feature of this van. I do not like the style of the newer vans and if I could get another van with the style of the 1991's I would probably trade mine in for a new one. I have four grand children I keep everyday and my van is just right for four baby car seats and still have extra room.  I would recommend the Town &amp; Country Mini Van to everyone.   Pat Cousins<br><br>
