            The Samsung Digimax 800K is the world's smallest digital camera with 800K Pixel Technology.  Designed for beginners,you get exactly what you want in a digital camera for a very low price.  For under $200.00 it possess an 800,000 pixel CCD and capable of 1024 x 768 (XVGA Resolution). Simply install the transfer software that comes with the camera, plug the camera into your PC and transfer the images from your camera to your PC.  One of the main features is the .8 megapixel picture resolution which can deliver an image in excess of 800,000 resolutions. The more pixels you have the more detailed your pictures will be. Samsung has really hit a home run with this one!<br><br>
