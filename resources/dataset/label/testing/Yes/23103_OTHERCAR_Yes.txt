            Polaroid PhotoMax PDC-640 is an exceptional camera at a very affordable price.  Found at any local Wal-Mart or other super store, this camera is made for users of all levels.  It is consumer friendly, requiring little electronic or computer knowledge.  It has easy-to-understand instructions and hardware that is a cinche to install.  Photos are excellent quality, clear and easy to download or delete!  Options are basic (like flash, no-flash, red-eye reduction, 3 variable distance selections, and 4 options for photo quality) but cover all necessities of great photography! Powered by 2 AA batteries or plug in to your outlet with included adapter.  Perfect for those who like to submit photos on auctions, keep in touch with family or for any creative idea that deserves being captured on &quot;film&quot;!
