 This digital comes with enough bells and whistles to make most people happy. With options for close-ups, flash or not, size of the saved picture, in camera picture review, auto picture timer, and even a spot to attach a tripod, users will be playing with the possibilities for quit some time.
 Pictures are sharp and the colors are very close to &quot;real&quot;. For use on the internet, they are ideal. Printing these photos with a &quot;good&quot; color printer on photo quality paper gives store bought results.
