            * The small stylish design is what first caught my eye. The Olympus Stylus 300 Digital Camera is small enough to fit in a shirt pocket, yet fits comfortably in your hand. The dimensions of the camera are 3.8" W x 2.2" H x 1.3" D and only weights 5.8 oz. without the batteries and memory card. The entire body is made of a light weight metal which provides excellent durability and style. This camera can be used in all weather conditions including rain and snow.

* 3.2 mega pixel resolution will allow you to print excellent quality, professional looking 8x10's. The 3x optical/4x digital zoom lens will let you get up close so you will never again miss that perfect shot. This camera will allow you to take panorama pictures with the supplied xD-Picture Card. Five scene programs to choose from will provide you with excellent images in all situations. Built in TruePic technology results in smooth, true color images. 

* QuickTime Movie Mode allows you to record video so you will never again have to carry around 2 cameras. With the supplied 16 mb memory card you are able to record 40 seconds of video and by purchasing additional memory cards you will virtually be able to record unlimited high-resolution video.

* There is absolutely no shutter lag. The shutter speed can be adjusted from 1/1000 sec. to 1/2 sec.

* The 1.5" LCD display to very easy to see and can be used to view and edit images.

* Everything you will ever need to produce high-quality, professional looking images is included in the box. Included: rechargeable LI-10B lithium ion batteries and charger, 16MB xD-picture card, self-timer remote control, USB cable, video cable, wrist strap, quick start guide, printed instruction manual. The included CD-ROM feature Camedi master software and Adobe Acrobat Reader.

* Built in red eye reduction flash.
* Auto power off

* The Olympus Stylus 300 Digital camera is so easy to use anyone will be able to operate it, yet it is packed with features for the professional photographer. The memory is expandable up to 256 MB by purchasing an additional xD-Picture Card.
