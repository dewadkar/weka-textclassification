            In the late 80's the S class ruled the luxury sector of the market. If you were seen in one of these you were important. The car has status that no BMW or Jag can match at this time. The S class is a classic looking car and is very pretty. Yet at the same time it looks daunting enough to scare you off the motorway. The 560 SEL was the one to have with its V8 providing Exelent performance. Buying the car in the year 2000 it may seem dated but at its time it was the most technologicly advanced car in the world. The smaller engines provide good fuel econemy yet with ample power.<br><br>
