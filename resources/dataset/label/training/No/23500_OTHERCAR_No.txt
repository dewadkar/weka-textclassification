            The Fuji MX-700 is an absolute breeze to use.
Sleek and very light the Fuji MX-700 makes taking your first digital photos a dream. With variable sizes of memory, and changeable resolution inside you can customize your shots as you like.
Just think about the savings in development, film stock and those crummy shots you're spending cash on now.
 And with a built in LCD screen you can see the finished product before the moment is over. 
The Fuji uses a micro memory card that allows you to keep on shooting just swap memory cards. With 5 different sizes of cards to choose from you don't need to run out in a big day's events.
Downloading to your PC or watching on your TV is a snap with the provided cords. Fuji provides you with software too, making all of your photos ready to print.
The Fuji MX-700 is all the camera you'll need and in a first time camera package.
