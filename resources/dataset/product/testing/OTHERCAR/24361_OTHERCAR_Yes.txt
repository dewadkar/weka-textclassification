            It's an easy-to-use toy that is great for beginners or advance users.

Basics: Upto 2.1 million pixel (Good Quality, only Olympus makes better)
JPEG Format pictures with varying sizes, and quality.  Short MPEG movies with sound.

Cool Features:  
&gt; Audio Visual Output - Plug straight to any TV's AV input and see you pics/movies on the TV.
&gt; Picture Gear Software is great with image organizer, thumbnails, etc.
&gt; Macro Function - Who needs a scanner when you have this for documents.

Features: 
&gt; Serial connection to PC is easy and quick.
&gt; Good quality pictures.
&gt; Visual Effect: Sepia, Black &amp; White
&gt; Navigates very easily, there is no need to read the instructions

BUT...:
&gt; Battery only lasts one hour because of the backlighting
&gt; Sony's proprietary Memory Sticks limitations: 16MB = twenty 15-Second MPEG movies.  JPEGs - 40 to 100 depending on quality of image
