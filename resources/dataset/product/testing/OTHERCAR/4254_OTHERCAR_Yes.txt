            BMW has a true hit with the 318 Series.  Newly redesigned, it upholds the proud BMW tradition of combining elegance with power.  It's strong engine takes the road like almost no other car, providing its driver with smooth, quiet acceleration, seemingly powerful enough to haul any 18-wheeler.  Service is certainly not a problem with the 318's.   Virtually maintenance free, it allows the driver to merrily explore the open road without worrying about anything more than the periodic oil change.  The interior boasts many standard gadgets that other cars offer as options costing well into the thousands of dollars.  It's sophisticated instrumentation cluster provide a playground for any gadget guru.  All in all, be sure to test 318 if you're in the luxury car market.<br><br>
