            This exciting new SUV, The 4Runner Limited, Is a great smooth ride anywhere.  Built on truck chasis, has four doors, a cargo bay, seats 5 people comfortably, and an option between two and four wheel drive. Toyota's midsize luxury SUV has four cylinders, and a 4x4 mode for off-road driving. Also includes a V6 engine, anti-lock brakes, 183 hp, and a coil-spring four-link system in the rear. An SR5 package puts exterior trim elements, power locks, mirrors, and tilt steering wheel. Also available are Aluminum alloy wheels, leather and wood trimings, and fender flares. This is a great SUV and a great smooth ride.<br><br>
