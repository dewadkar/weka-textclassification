Partial list as follows:

1.Power steering pump/clamp failed TWICE, even after recall repair.
2.Side mirror replaced (motor)
3.Drivers seat track mechanism replaced
4.Side mirror adjust knobs replaced (twice)
5.Automatic door locking mechanism,rear door latch replaced
6.Brakes replaced (twice) due to excessive screeching...told it was due 
   to cold weather)but happened in summer as well.
7.Driver window motor replaced
8. Battery failed without cause (i.e.nothing left on accidentally) while car  
was less than 1 year old.
9. ABS/SRS light and/or systems failed repeatedly, warning lights come on constantly, we never know if the air bags/abs brakes are really working or not,repaired several times.
