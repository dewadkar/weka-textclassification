            The Ram Series of Dodge trucks have long been among the leaders when it comes to full size trucks.  The style is unique , it have lots of room and a useful Quad Cab.

INTERIOR
Instruments and controls are a glances away.  Front and rear viewing are exceptional including the large side view mirrors.  The cup holders flip down out of the instrument panel and adjust themselves to secure cups of various size.  The overhead console includes finger-touch reading lamps, outside temperature display as well as a digital compass.  The center console can hold everything from CDs and cassettes to cell phone and laptop computers.  The 40/20/40 split bench seat carries three adult comfortably.  Driver and passenger seats recline.  In addition, the rear bench seat fold up for a large storage area.

EXTERIOR
A variety of colors to choose from.  The unique Dodge style set it apart from other trucks.  Include four doors  on the body.  The bed is 49.3 inches wide between wheelhouses.   Running boards and rails are optional.  However,  some models includes the fog lights.

UNDER THE HOOD
A powerful  5.9-liter(360) Magnum V8 engine.   Lot of room to work.  All the fluids ( oil, power steering,coolant, etc,)  are color coded for easy recognition.  Includes a automatic transmission and a towing package for Classes I - III.

THE TEST DRIVE
Easy to handle with a smooth ride.  Turns are sharp with little movement on the driver.  acceleration and passing  is quick with no jerky movements. No wind noise or rattles.                   <br><br>
