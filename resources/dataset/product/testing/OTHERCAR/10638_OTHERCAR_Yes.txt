            *Get excellent quality, professional pictures with the HP Photosmart 720 digital camera.  Simple to use yet packed with every feature imaginable. 

*3.3 MP total resolution for professional quality prints with the ability for enlargements up to 11 x 14 inches with no or little loss of quality. 12x total zoom, 3x optical and 4x digital zoom for excellent quality close-ups.

*A built in feature lets you record a sound clip to go along with your pictures and video clips.

*16mb of internal memory to store your pictures and video clips. The built in memory card slot also allows you to add an unlimited amount of extra memory to store extra pictures and video clips without having to remove your old pictures first using a memory card (sold separately).

*The HP Photosmart 8881 digital camera dock (sold separately) allows you to easily and quickly connect your HP Photosmart 720 digital camera to your television, for a large view of the pictures you have taken, and to your computer for easy uploading and editing. The dock also allows you to recharge your cameras rechargeable batteries (included with the dock) saving you a ton of money by not having to purchase those expensive non-rechargeable camera batteries.

*The included HP memories disc creator software is excellent for creating your own digital slide shows and then viewing them on your PC or TV. The included HP photo & imaging software is very easy to use and learn. The software application makes editing all your pictures easy and fun and can be used with all your pictures, not just the pictures taken with your digital camera.

*Taking pictures is easy with the auto exposure, red-eye reduction and auto flash. The easy to see 1.6 inch color LCD screen makes centering your picture very easy. There is also an optical view finder if you prefer the &#147old fashioned way&#148. 

*The HP Photosmart 720 digital camera is excellent for everyone form a teenager to a professional. Everything is so simple to use that it will take you less then five minutes to learn how to use it.
