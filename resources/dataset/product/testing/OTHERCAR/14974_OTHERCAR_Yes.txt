            The Sony Digital Mavica FD-73
We have had ours for 1 year now...
It works flawlessly...
Its easy to use, and is so lightweight its amazing...
It weighs only about 1 1/2 lbs...
Features, it has them, and if you are a novice or an expert, you can set the features as you want them...
At a price you can afford...
You can find it on the net for about $555.00
It is easy to use...
And the photos are great...
The only problem is the red eye problem with flash photography...
