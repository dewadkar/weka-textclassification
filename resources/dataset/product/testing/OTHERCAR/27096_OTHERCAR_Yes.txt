            Every teen would love to get their hands on this beauty.  It's fast, looks great, and perfect for you and that special someone.  However, not too many people feel that they would ever get the chance to sit behind the wheel of a Viper and be allowed to drive away legally.  Well, I got my chance.

It was bought for me for a graduation present.  The one phrase that would sum it up is that everyone was jealous.  Owning one of these makes you feel important and in the top class of society.  I don't have to worry about if I'm going to get to one place to another or if I'm going to be late.  I highly recommend this car to those who love Dodge, like me.  If you ever have the chance of buying a Viper or a different vehicle, do yourself a favor and buy the Viper.<br><br>
