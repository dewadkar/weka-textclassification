            While shopping the endless market of SUV's, we decided to check out the Infiniti Qx4.  We were impressed first by its exterior sleekness.  The next step was to experience how it felt to get into the vehicle on the driver's side.  My husband and I had tried several different styles and models of SUV's, and discovered climbing in to several had been a real chore!  With the Infiniti, this was pleasant!  This hurdle jumped, we proceeded to test-drive the Qx4 and liked how it felt on the road.  We have owned cars, trucks, and vans, yet this has been our most favorite vehicle.  We have driven it for one year, just returned from a lengthy vacation, and have not experienced any problem at all (knock on wood     Try it; you'll like it!
