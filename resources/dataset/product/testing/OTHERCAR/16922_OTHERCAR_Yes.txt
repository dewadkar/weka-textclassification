            What a car!  This wagon put put by subaru gives you versatility of a sports utility vehicle with the feel of a car.  This wagon handles superbly and rides quietly and comfortably.  There is plenty of room to fit five adults and incredible cargo space in back.  Put down the split back seats and your cargo space increase. Rain, snow, sleet, it doesn't matter.  This car handles equally well under all conditions thanks to its all wheel drive. Even gas mileage is better than in actual sport utility trucks. This car is perfect for families of even for the college students who needs the cargo space going back and forth to school.  Its in a well rounded vehicle that makes wagons look fashionable.  The Subaru outback is paving the way for a new line of sport utility vehicles.<br><br>
