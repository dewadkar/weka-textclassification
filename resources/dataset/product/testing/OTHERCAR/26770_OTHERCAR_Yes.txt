                When I began looking for a car, I considered many kinds of sport-utility vehicles. However, my eyes continued to lead me back to the Jeep Cherokees, a very trusted name in the  industry. I went to a local dealership and laid my eyes on my new set of wheels. Needless to say, it was love at first sight. These Jeeps have lots of cargo space, which was an important factor for this college student. However, because of all the cargo space,room in the back seat is very limited. (If any of you have friends with long legs, consider letting them ride in the front seat!) The sound system is absolutely superb, and the ride is fairly smooth. 

           Unfortunately, my Jeep has revealed itself to be a complete gas guzzler, which is a problem with many sports-utes. Also, my car has been in the shop at least six times in the past eight months, Now, take that as you may, but I don't consider that a sign of ruggedness.
