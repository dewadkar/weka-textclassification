            There is so much to say about the Mercedes-Benz M-Class.  It is a wonderful addition to the already remarkable line of Mercedes vehicles.  The M-Class is a wise investment for anyone interested in purchasing a sport utility vehicle.  It boasts the many different features that are fit for rugged off-road adventure while also providing the comfort of the riding and handling available in Mercedes cars.  This luxurious sport utility vehicle features a unique 4-wheel, 4-channel anti-lock brake system.  The M-Class also boasts the electronic traction system.  

Besides the M-Class excellence in performance, it also excels in luxury.  It has 8-way power front seats with seat memory, radio-frequency remote control locking system, automatic antitheft alarm system, remote-activated illuminated entry system, integrated 3-button garage door open, electronic cruise control, auto-dimming rearview mirror with dual focused map lamps, delayed-fadeout courtesy lamps, covered auxiliry 12-volt power outlets, lockable drawer-type safebox, leather upholstery, leather-trimmed steering wheel, burl walnut shift knob, velour carpeting, Modular Control System, 6-disc CD changer, built in digital cellular phone, on-board GPS satellite navigation, Integrated Restraint System, Emergency Tensioning Retractor, Babysmart, and digital trip computer just to name a few.  It is easy to see that for the money the M-Class is a steal.  It is ideal for anyone from the off-road adventurer to the business person or to the family.<br><br>
