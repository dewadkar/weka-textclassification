            The 2000 Contour is a 4-door, 5-passenger midsize sedan available in one trim only. Its closest competitors include the Nissan Altima, Volkswagen Jetta, Pontiac Grand Am and Chevrolet Malibu. According to Ford, &quot;Contour offers shoppers an attractive blend of responsive driving dynamics, upscale styling and sporty performance.&quot; Upon introduction, the Contour is equipped with a standard 2.5-liter, V6, 170-horsepower engine that consume 20-mpg in the city and 29-mpg on the highway. A 5-speed manual transmission with overdrive is standard and a 4-speed automatic transmission with overdrive is optional. The 2000 Contour is a carryover from 1999. New standard features include a Securilock(tm) vehicle anti-theft system, variable-speed interval windshield wipers, remote keyless entry, power antenna, fog lights, and added colors. <br><br>
