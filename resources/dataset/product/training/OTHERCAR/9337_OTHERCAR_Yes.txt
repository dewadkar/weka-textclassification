            SUVs have become the new hot item of the auto industry.  There are many options available when searching for one.  The LX 470 provides luxury like no other SUV.  Additionally, it was designed to handle like a car.  Its quiet ride and reliability fit the Lexus trademark.  The safety guaranteed by Lexus cannot be matched anywhere.  Yet, all these features come with a huge price tag.  If one has the money, I definitely recommend the LX 470.  It is basically like a car in an intimidating frame.  Basically, if you want to be the hotshot of your neighborhood, this car's for you.<br><br>
