            Bmw continues their excellence for crisp engineering as they developed another award winner for the 2000  as they introduced the new 3 series coupe.  It's powered by a double vanos 2.5 inline six, pumping out 170 horses@5500rpm and 181 lb.-ft@3500rpm.  Different from the sedan, the coupes gains standard sports suspension for a more stiff, structured ride which offers great handling and awesome cornering.  Smooth, creamy, powerful, and luxurious best describes this new sports coupe from BMW as everyone got a hint on the next M3 which is due for 2001.  This car is great in handling the road as you can really make razor sharp turns while the steering wheel feeds the feel of smooth pavement right into your palms.  This car offers so much with less out of your pocket, making it more affordable for everyone that wants a good run for their money.  By adding a sweet gear shift, powerful engine, awesome brakes, and grippy handling, you will definetly feel like a race car driver everytime you turn on the engine of a 3 series coupe.  
Technical: 0-60 in 7.1 sec.  top speed : 128mph
